Chocolate Chip Banana Nut Bread

Serves:20

Takes 90 min

This bread was inspired by my love for banana bread and my brothers love for putting chocolate and nuts in everything. So after a few trial and error runs we finally came up with this.

  • Ingredients
    • 3 cups all purpose flour
    • 3/4 teaspoons salt
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1 cup chopped pecans or walnuts
    • 3 whole eggs, beaten
    • 1/2 cup apple sauce
    • 3 cups mashed ripe bananas
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions

1. Combine first 5 ingrediants, stir in pecans.

2. Combine remaining ingrediants; add flour to mixture, stirring just intil dry ingredients are moistened.

3. Spoon batter into 2 greased and floured loaf pans.

4. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour 10 minutes or untill test done.

5. Cool in pans for 10 minutes; remove from pans and cool on wire racks. Enjoy!

Chocolate No-Bake Cookies

Serves: 32

Prep/Cook: 5min/5min

These cookies were inspired by my younger sisters passion to bake, so we found a slightly more kid friendly recipe for her to have fun with.

  • Ingregients
    • 1 3/4 Cups granulated sugar
    • 3 Tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 1/2 Cup milk
    • 8Tbsp butter
    • 1/4 Tsp vanilla extract
    • 1/2 Cup peanut butter
    • 3 Cups quick cooking oats

Instructions

1. Mix sugar and cocoa powder in sauce pan.

2. add milk and butter then bring sauce pan to a rolling boil.

3. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla, salt, peanut butter, and oats.

4. Scoop small cookies onto parchment paper and let cool 10-30min Enjoy!

Cake Mix Cinnamon Rolls

Serves: 24

Prep/Cook: 1hr 10min/25min

These were inspired by my family always having cooking copetitions, after a while we decided they can't get much better than this. So feel free to share this with you family and friends.

  • Ingredients
    • 1 Box of white or yellow cake mix
    • 2 Packages of active dry yeast
    • 2 1/2 Cups of warm water
    • 1 Teaspoon vanilla
    • 1 Teaspoon salt
    • 5 Cups flour
    • Filling:
    • 2 Cups brown sugar
    • As much cinnamon as you desire
    • 1/2 Cups butter, softened
    • Frosting:
    • 1 Can cream cheese frosting

Instructions

1. Mix yeast and warm water until dissolved.

2. Combine cake mix, salt, and flour in a large bowl.

3. Add warm water/yeast mixture and vanilla to cake mix, salt, and flour mixture.(Mix well)

4. Cover tightly, let rise for one hour. punch down and let rise again for another 30-60min.

5. On a floured surface, roll dough into a rectangle shape approx 1/4 inch thick.

6. Once dough is rolled out, brush with softened butter, sprinkle w/ cinnamon & brown sugar.

7. Starting from one end roll the dough into one long piece. once in one long piece slice into 24pcs.

8. Place rolls in 2 greased 9x13 pans, cover pans and let dough rise to twice its size.

9. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees

10. Bake at 350 degrees at 15-25 minutes or until golden brown.

11. Remove from oven and let cool for 10-15min, apply icing and Enjoy!
